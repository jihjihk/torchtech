Why This App is Important?

Traffic jams cost Qatar between QR5.6 and 6.6 billion in 2016, which was about 0.9-1 percent of the GDP in 2016, according to Qatar Traffic Report (QTR). The figure was QR5.2-6 billion for 2015. The report says commuters spent an average of 109 extra hours on roads due to traffic congestion in Qatar in 2016 as against 102 extra hours in 2015. You are one of those people who get affected by trafic.

Our application is trying to help you by utalizing yout time, cost and stress. Using Viable, you can reach you distination without the stress of traffic, finding parking or arriving late. With our analysis tool we are able to predict the right time for you to leave distination A to reach Distination B.
